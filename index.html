About Me


I am a fourth year Environmental Science major and a Full-stack web developer student at University of California, Irvine. The love of nature and passion for technology have driven me to pursue a bachelor degree in Environmental Science and to gain a new skill set of technology as a web developer.


I am a student intern for Center for Environmental Biology for UCI school of Biological Science and a chemical inventory student worker at Environmental Health and Safety at UCI.


I have experienced working with UCI scientists and local land managers in Orange County through Ecological Restoration Project and Research Studies. My current research project focuses on Traits of Common Native Plant Species of the Coastal Sage Scrub Community, in which I conducted the experiment on 18 common native species in Mediterranean Climate System.


I also have experienced with web development tools including HTML, CSS, Javascript, React.js, Node.js, MySQL, Bootstap, Materialize,Firebase, and jQuery.


Additionally, I am learning some new technological skills that are extensively used in analyzing data such as Geographical Information System (GIS), Data Analysis using MATLAB, and R-Studio.
